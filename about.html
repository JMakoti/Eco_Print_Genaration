About Us

Turning recycled materials into sustainable 3D innovations.

Our Story

We transform plastic waste into affordable 3D-printing filaments and classroom-ready STEM models—making hands-on learning accessible while cleaning our environment.

From oceans and landfills to labs and schools, EcoPrint Generation connects circular materials with real education impact.

Our Mission

EcoPrint Generation’s mission is to transform plastic waste into sustainable 3D printing materials that empower education, innovation, and environmental conservation—turning waste into opportunity for a cleaner, smarter, and more inclusive future.

Our Vision

A continent where every learner can touch, see, and build science— powered by sustainable materials and community innovation.

  • Lower cost, higher access
  • Materials from recycled plastics
  • Practical STEM for real schools

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We turn waste into opportunity through eco-friendly 3D printing.

Innovation

We create smart, modern solutions that drive positive change.

Education

We empower learners and creators through training and mentorship.

Community

We collaborate to build a greener, more connected future.

Our Impact

Quality Education (SDG 4)

We create learning tools and STEM models for classrooms, expanding access to quality education.

Decent Work & Growth (SDG 8)

Local collection, processing, and manufacturing create jobs and sustainable livelihoods.

Responsible Consumption (SDG 12)

By turning plastic waste into products, we reduce waste and promote circular consumption.
