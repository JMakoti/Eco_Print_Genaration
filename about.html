About Us

Turning recycled materials into sustainable 3D innovations.

Our Story

We transform plastic waste into affordable 3D-printing filaments and classroom-ready STEM models—making hands-on learning accessible while cleaning our environment.

From oceans and landfills to labs and schools, EcoPrint Generation connects circular materials with real education impact.

EcoPrint Generation’s mission is to transform plastic waste into sustainable 3D printing materials that empower education, innovation, and environmental conservation—turning waste into opportunity for a cleaner, smarter, and more inclusive future.

A continent where every learner can touch, see, and build science— powered by sustainable materials and community innovation.
