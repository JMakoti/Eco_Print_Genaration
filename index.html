Our Story

EcoPrint Generation is a youth‑led social enterprise changing how sustainability and education intersect. We believe real innovation lifts communities, not leaves them behind. Our work focuses on two urgent challenges: plastic pollution and limited access to STEM learning in underserved schools.

We collect discarded plastics from beaches, landfills, and neighborhoods and give them a new life as 3D printing filament. That filament is then used to create hands-on, curriculum-aligned STEM models for students who rarely get to experience practical science. Our approach is practical, scalable, and deeply human. Every piece of plastic we reclaim from the environment is a step toward a smarter classroom, a cleaner planet, and a more empowered generation

Our Vision

A continent where every learner can touch, see, and build science— powered by sustainable materials and community innovation.
