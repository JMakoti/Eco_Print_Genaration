Our Services

Eco-Friendly 3D Filament

We manufacture and sell recycled 3D printing filament in various colors and formats — affordable, sustainable, and ideal for education or prototyping.

STEM Education Kits

We produce curriculum-aligned 3D models in science, math, and biology — anatomical models, lab equipment, geometric solids, and more — designed for schools in low-resource settings.

Custom 3D Printing Services

We offer affordable prototyping for innovators, schools, and organizations needing unique, high-quality models.

Environmental Education Programs

We host beach cleanups, workshops, and youth engagements to build awareness on sustainability, recycling, and innovation.

Partnerships & Pilots

We collaborate with NGOs, CSR programs, and education initiatives to co-develop impactful sustainability programs and pilots.

Community Empowerment

We empower youth and women-led groups through training in recycling, digital fabrication, and creative design for social good.
